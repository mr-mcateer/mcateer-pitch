The Path Here

2014–2020 | Lebanon, Oregon

Cold-called a CTE director. Got a part-time science position. Transformed it into a comprehensive CTE program—secured grants, installed 3D printers, CAD/CAM systems, and advanced manufacturing equipment. Built it from nothing.

2020 | The Move

Relocated to Corvallis. Second child on the way. Lebanon commute became untenable. Despite credentials and cold calls to every principal in the district, no CTE positions existed locally.

2024 | Crescent Valley High School

Hired at 0.5 FTE. Inherited a metals program with years of turnover. No documentation. Idle equipment. Within months: revived the shop, launched a student store, began pursuing CSWA certification for students.

Today | 0.5 FTE

Underutilized. Half the time. Half the impact. The other half goes to consulting work that doesn’t need me as much as these students do.
